There are two challenges within Competitive mode:

  • Declension test - enter the declension for Latin nouns.
  • Vocabulary test - translate words from Latin to English.

You have two minutes to complete as many as possible, but if you get even one word wrong, your turn will end! At the end, you can submit your name to be included on the Leaderboard. Please select your exam board at the top before beginning the test.
